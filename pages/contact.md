We would love to hear from you. Whether you have a question, found something we got wrong, want a scam flagged, or have a partnership idea, send it our way.

## Email

The fastest way to reach us is email: **hello@firstpaycheck.co**

We read every message. We are a small operation, so give us a little time, but a real person will get back to you.

## Spotted a scam?

If you got a pitch or "job offer" that felt off, two things help. First, run it through the free [Scam Smell Test](/) for an instant read. Second, if it is a new pattern we should warn people about, email it over and we may feature it.

## For coaches and communities

If you run a legitimate work-from-home community or coaching program and want honest tools as a member benefit, we are open to partnerships. Email us with a little about your audience.

The best way to keep up with new openings, fresh scam alerts, and what is actually working is the free newsletter. You can sign up on the [home page](/). No hype, unsubscribe anytime.
